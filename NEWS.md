# aisdk.providers 0.1.0

* First release. Adds additional AI model provider adapters for the
  `aisdk` toolkit, covering OpenAI-compatible and Anthropic-compatible
  services (DeepSeek, Moonshot/Kimi, Stepfun, Volcengine, AiHubMix, xAI,
  OpenRouter, Bailian and NVIDIA). Providers register themselves with the
  core `aisdk` provider registry on load.
